Introduction

What's Play with Shakespeare

The website offers digital editions of Shakespeare’s plays, suited for children.

The ideal range of age of the children developing the activities would be 6-11 years, but of course the users are free to decide if our project could adapt for other children according to their abilities and competences.

The website has an intended audience of teachers, educators, animators, parents, who could use the website as material for children's activities, thus, the layout is clear and easily understandable.

The texts of the plays are short and selected in order to be interesting for an audience of young listeners. They contain only a simplified version of the raw dialogues, becoming a story which a child could easily read.

The main activity proposed by the website is the children's recital of the plays in order to make them learn about Shakespeare’s creations and characters in a fun and engaging way but the texts could also be read as stories.

All the additional information, for example about the characters, the scenes of the play, the moves of the characters on the scene, will be expressed through markup (XML), so that just the adults approaching this website will be using them to obtain more information according to the different needs and situations experienced.
